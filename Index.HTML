puerto rico: a beautiful island

I visited puerto rico last month, and it was such an awesome experience!

According to Wikitravel, "Puerto Rico is a Caribbean island that is a self-governing commonwealth of the United States of America. Located in the Caribbean Sea to the east of the Dominican Republic and west of the US Virgin Islands, Puerto Rico lies on a key shipping lane to the Panama Canal, the Mona Passage." I say that this beautiful island is worth a visit, and that's all you need to know!
